My Biography


MY LIFE MY RULES, MY STYLE MY ATTITUDE

My name is Ramazan. I am 18 years old. I was born on October 15, 2004 in the city of Taraz, Zhambyl region, Kazakhstan. I am a first-year student in cybersecurity at Astana IT University. I graduated from a specialized school for gifted children with education in three languages in the city of Taraz. I was an excellent student and received a red certificate

When I was at school, I took part in various olympiads. I have achievements as the winner of a prize in the Presidential Olympiad, the winner of a prize in the Republican and International Olympiad for the protection of the project. I like to spend my free time on music. I listen and play songs on the guitar. In addition to the guitar, I play the dombra and perform Kazakh national kuyi. I also want to learn how to play the piano.
